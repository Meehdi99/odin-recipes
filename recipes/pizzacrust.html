Pizza Crust

pizza crust recipe was given to my by a friend, it is quick,easy, and delicious

Ingredients:

  • 1 package active dry yeast
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 cups bread flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Steps:

  1. Dissolve yeast in warm water, about 5 minutes.
  2. Combine bread flour,olive oil, sugar, salt, and yeast mixture; stir well, cover the bowl, about 30 minutes.
  3. Preheat the over to 350 degrees F.
  4. Turn dough out onto a well-floured surface. Cover with your favorite sauce and toppings (not pineapple please).
  5. Bake pizza in the preheated oven until crust is golden brown, about 20 minutes.
