Teriyaki Meatballs

An easy and delicious twist on meatballs! These teriyaki meatballs can be served over rice, or pasta, or even a chewy French bread roll for sub sandwiches.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/4 cup bread crumbs
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoons garlic, minced
  • 2 large egg whites

Steps

  1. Mix all the ingredients in a bowl until well combined. Form into 1-ounce balls. Transfer the prepared baking sheet.
  2. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 160 degrees F.
  3. Preheat the over to 350 degrees F.
  4. Meanwhile prepare a sauce to your liking
  5. Stir and simmer the sauce, add meatballs and cook until heated through, about 5 minutes.
